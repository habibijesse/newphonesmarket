Someone so precious is celebrating a birthday today..

Dear Love,

Life can be so cruel sometimes.
It puts you in such a bliss only to tear you apart when you feel
like you're just an inch away from perfect happiness.
It teaches you lessons without a thought whatsoever
if your life- worth of joy would come with it.
It offers you love with open hand
as if saying , "here take it."
only to snatch it away, when it already
sits perfectly in your arms.
But life can be so sweet too.
It brings you lullabies, and new laughter
from someone you love.
It's generous with friendships,
most of them staying for too long, even in darkest hours.
It teaches you love, and distance and things in between.
I believe God teared a page out of someone's perfect story book
and slipped it into mine.
And you came with it.
Dimpled and talented.
And compassioante and loving.
I love you. Happy birthday.
Love,
Jessa
